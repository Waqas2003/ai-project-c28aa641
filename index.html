For Every Child

UNICEF works in over 190 countries to save children's lives, to defend their rights, and to help them fulfill their potential.

About UNICEF

UNICEF is the world's largest provider of vaccines for developing countries, saving 2-3 million lives every year.

Our Programs

  • Education

    We work to ensure that all children, regardless of their background or circumstances, have access to quality education.

  • Health

    We work to improve the health and well-being of children, including through immunization, nutrition, and disease prevention.

  • Protection

    We work to protect children from violence, exploitation, and abuse, and to ensure their rights are respected.
